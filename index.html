128 Academia Road, Section 2, Nankang, Taipei 115, Taiwan· sypdbhee@citi.sinica.edu.tw

Vita：
I received the B.S. (2008) and M.S. (2010) degrees in department of electrical engineering from National Changhua University of Education and National Chi Nan University, respectively. After one year service for military, I joined to the research group of Dr. Yu Tsao in the Research Center for Information Technology Innovation (CITI) at Academia Sinica till now. I am a PhD. student from 2013 and study in the department of Graduate Institute of Communication Engineering at National Taiwan University. My adviser is Dr. Borching Su.
Since I was studying the M.S. degree, discrete wavelet transform (DWT) was applied to extract robust speech features and to increase the recognized efficiency of an auto-speech recognition (ASR) system. Several researched results with extended works were published and won one excellent paper award at TAAI. Recently, I am involved in the research of applying deep learning techniques to speech coding, speech synthesis/enhancement, robust feature extractions and the sound-based biomedicine signal processing.

簡歷：
2008年在彰化師大電機工程學系畢業，並在同年進入暨南大學電機研究所系統組；隨後於2010年獲得碩士學位。一年的兵役後，我加入曹昱 博士的研究團隊，在中研院資創中心，並延續至今。自2013年起，進入台大電信所博士班就讀，指導教授為蘇柏青博士。
攻讀碩士學位時，我的研究主題為運用離散小波分解於擷取強健語音特徵，以提升自動語音辨識系統的辨識效能。其研究成果與其延申已發表於多篇論文；其中一篇發表於人工智慧與應用研討會中的論文得到優秀論文獎。除了語音辨識外，我的研究主題亦包含語音訊號壓縮、運用深層類非神經網路等深層學習的方式於語音合成與增強，另外有基於聲音之生理訊號處理，如發聲障礙之語音訊號強化等。由上所述，我的專長主要是語音訊號處理。
